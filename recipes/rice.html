Spanish Rice Recipe

How to make Spanish Rice

The best and easiest Spanish rice!

Ingredients:

  • Uncooked White Rice
  • Minced Garlic
  • Water
  • Jar of Salsa

Steps:

  1. Combine the rice with the garlic in a large saucepan.
  2. Pour water and salsa into the rice mixture.
  3. Bring the water to a full boil and then reduce the temperature to simmer.
  4. Simmer for 20 minutes or until the rice is tender. Fluff the rice when finished.
